About Me

I was born and raised in Bandarwela,Sri Lanaka. Currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Software Engineering at the University of Westminster. I have a rich background in both academics and extracurricular activities, having been an active student and head girl at B/Kuda Kusum Balika National School. My passion for technology has led me to gain hands-on experience with Java, Spring Boot, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and React.

Beyond technical skills, I excel in communication, teamwork, and leadership, which I honed during my tenure as head girl and through various group projects. I am highly adaptable, a quick learner, and always eager to embrace new challenges. Additionally, I have a love for indoor planting and dancing, which keep me balanced and creative. I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge in real-world projects and continuously seek opportunities to learn and grow in the field of software engineering.
